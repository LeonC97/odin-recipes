Mushroom Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Kosher salt
  • 1.5 pounds crimini or button mushrooms
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 2 medium shallots
  • 1 cup parsley
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 oz grated parmesan
  • 1.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • .25 cup EVOO
  • 1 pound orecchiette

Steps

  1. Boil salty water
  2. discard mushroom stems, quarter mushrooms
  3. slice garlic, dice shallots, chop parsley
  4. whisk egg yolks, large egg, parmesan, and pepper
  5. med-high, saute mushrooms in EVOO, toss every 5 minutes for 15 minutes, mushroom should be golden brown
  6. boil pasta, 2 min shy of al dente
  7. reduce heat to med-low, add garlic, shallots, and salt< (1 min)/li>
  8. save 2 cups of pasta water
  9. add pasta and 1 cup of pasta water to mushroom mixture, cook about 2 min, remove from heat
  10. slowly add .5 cup of pasta water to egg mixture and whisk to combine
  11. add egg mix to pasta, mix
  12. add parsley and stir
  13. top with parmesan and pepper
